# DTC_Skincare_Analysis

Executive Summary


1. Capitalize on seasonal upticks in November and December, provide free samples of hero products (exfoliators and serums) to encourage repeat purchases from new customers.
2. Incorporate a summer campaign that aligns with both the EOY sales volume and purchase frequency.
3. Create and promote product subscriptions to repeat customers in order to increase CLV.

Business Problem
Analyze 24 months of sales data for a DTC skincare brand and created a dashboard with sales information and KPIs in order to identify opportunities for revenue growth.

Methodology
1. Chat GPT generated dataset in order to utilize data that mimics realistic data
2. A dashboard in Tableau that tracks KPIs such as total revenue, Average Order Value, Purchase Frequency, Customer Lifetime Value

Skills Used
Tableau: writing functions, ETL, data visualization,

Results and Recommendations 


1. Capitalize on seasonal upticks in November and December, provide free samples of hero products (exfoliators and serums) to encourage repeat purchases from new customers.
2. Incorporate a summer campaign that aligns with both the EOY sales volume and purchase frequency.
3. Create and promote product subscriptions to repeat customers in order to increase CLV.

Next Steps
1. Incorporate more marketing data in order to better understand campaign impact on sales (ROAS).
